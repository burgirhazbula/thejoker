Puns

Here are some puns to tickle your funny bone:

  • Why did the bicycle fall over? It was two-tired!

One-Liners

Enjoy these short and snappy one-liners:

  • I used to play piano by ear, but now I use my hands.

Knock-Knock Jokes

Have fun with these classic knock-knock jokes:

  • Knock, knock. Who's there? Lettuce. Lettuce who? Lettuce in, it's cold out here!
  • Knock, knock. Who's there? Boo. Boo who? Don't cry, it's just a joke!
  • Knock, knock. Who's there? Olive. Olive who? Olive you and I miss you!

Blonde Jokes

Enjoy these lighthearted blonde jokes:

  • Why did the blonde stare at the can of orange juice? Because it said "concentrate"!
  • How do you keep a blonde busy for hours? Give her a piece of paper with "please turn over" written on both sides!
  • Why don't blondes make good pharmacists? They can't figure out how to fit the bottle into the typewriter!
